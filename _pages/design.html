Design

Design and UX is a key part of what we do. It works best as an integral part of the overall process

You've probably got an idea of where you'd like to get to. Typically this might be a commercial version of your MVP. Something good enough to pay for.

To get to that point you're going to need quite a few things:

  • good project management,
  • to make the right technical + product decisions,
  • choose the right technology,
  • and create a user experience that works.

You need a well rounded team that can work together to deliver each of those aspects.
